About Silverstar

Silverstar is a premier luxury property management company focusing on providing the most luxurious Telluride vacation rentals, including condos, townhomes and mountain lodges with five-star accommodations, high-end amenities and impeccable customer service to each and every one of our guests. Silverstar offers over 125 luxury rental properties in the Telluride and Mountain Village areas with lodging options from 2 to 12 people.

Our most loyal guests are a testament to our many years of experience and attention to detail. The focus of Silverstar is a commitment to providing top of the line amenities and services to each of our guests during every stay. This is what sets Silverstar Luxury Properties apart from other lodging companies.
