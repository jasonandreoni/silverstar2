Summer In
Telluride

As the ski season ends and the snows starts to disappear, Telluride transforms into one of the most spectacular venues for live music and arts festivals in the country.

To help plan your unique getaway, check out our activities page here.

The season kicks off with the renowned Mountainfilm festival in May. Celebrating its 35th birthday, Mountainfilm is one of the longest-running film festivals in the country and brings filmmakers from around the world to Telluride to showcase their latest works.

Want to see Telluride from a totally different view? The Telluride Balloon Festival takes off in the beginning of June and allows you to watch hot air balloons lift off from Town Park in Telluride and float over the valley for the entire weekend.

Is live music more your speed? Join us this summer for the Telluride Bluegrass Festival, The Ride Festival, Telluride Jazz Festival or the Telluride Blues and Brews Festival, all of which bring nationally touring acts to Telluride for an amazing outdoor live music experience that is attended by thousands of people every summer.

Events aren’t the only thing that brings people to Telluride every summer. The outdoors is one of Colorado’s greatest treasures and inspires people from all over the world to visit Telluride and Mountain Village. With over 300 days of sun throughout the year, you can be assured you will rarely be rained out or stuck inside. Whether you are looking to fly fish in the San Miguel River that flows through town, playing a round of golf with spectacular views at the Mountain Village golf course or rafting down the rivers with one of Telluride’s guided tours, we have something everyone in the family can enjoy.

These are just a few of the activities available to you in Telluride and Mountain Village during the summer months. If there is something special that you want experience during your Telluride vacation, please let us know and we will do everything possible to make sure your stay with us unforgettable.
