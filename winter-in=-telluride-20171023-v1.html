Winter In
Telluride

Winter in Telluride can be an absolutely breathtaking experience. From the snow-covered mountains to the traditional holiday torch run down the slopes, planning a winter vacation with us can be something your family will never forget.

To help plan your unique getaway, check out our activities page here.

Telluride Ski Resort is the main attraction from Thanksgiving to Easter. Tens of thousands of skiers and snowboarders descend upon our mountain town to ride on of the Top 10 Mountains in the entire country. With over 2,000 ski-able acres and 127 unique trails, Telluride offers an experience that is perfect for the entire family. When you book your vacation with Silverstar, we can purchase your ski passes and lessons in advance so you can head right out your front door and onto the slopes without having to wait in line at the ticket counter. In addition to some of the best skiing and snowboarding in the country, Telluride also offers a wide array of other winter activities that are fun for the whole family.

Cross Country Skiing is also hugely popular among our guests during the winter. There are hundreds of miles of Nordic ski trails in and around Telluride. The Telluride Nordic Association offers up-to-date grooming reports for all of the trails in the Telluride area and offers important information or those who want to experience the best cross country skiing conditions available.

If this is going to be your first time visiting us in Telluride, a guided tour may be the best way to see all that the area has to offer. In town you have the ability to book guided snowmobile tours that will bring you through the fresh powder to tour the ghost towns, hot springs our just ride the trails to take in the breathtaking views of the mountain peaks and Telluride skyline. Need something more peaceful? Snowshoeing is a great way to explore the scenic landscapes and enjoy a relaxing winter morning with your friends and family.

These are just a few of the hundreds of winter activities available to you in Telluride and Mountain Village. If there is something special that you want experience during your Telluride vacation, please let us know and we will do everything possible to make sure your stay with us is full of memories you will talk about for years and years.
